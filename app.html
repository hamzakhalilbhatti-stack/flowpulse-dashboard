How to Add a New Client
Step 1 — Go to workers.cloudflare.com, create new Worker, paste worker.js
Step 2 — Update ALLOWED_ORIGINS with your domain
Step 3 — Add KV binding named FLOWPULSE_KV, deploy
Step 4 — Click "+ Add Client" and paste the Worker URL
Step 5 — Copy the Webhook URL → give to client for their n8n
Step 6 — Share their status page URL so they can monitor their flows
