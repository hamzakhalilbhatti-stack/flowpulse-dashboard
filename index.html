❓ FAQ
Common questions
Does this work with n8n Cloud trial? ↓
Yes! FlowPulse works with n8n trial accounts. You just need to add an HTTP Request node to your workflows — no n8n API access required.
Do I need to change my existing workflows? ↓
Minimal change. You just add one HTTP Request node at the end of each workflow. It takes about 2 minutes per workflow and doesn't affect anything else.
Where is my data stored? ↓
Your account is stored in Supabase (secure cloud database). Your workflow execution data is stored in your own private Cloudflare Worker KV — we never see it.
What is the Cloudflare Worker for? ↓
The Cloudflare Worker acts as a private middleman. Your n8n workflows send data to it, and your dashboard reads from it. It's completely free on Cloudflare's free tier.
Can I cancel my subscription? ↓
Yes, anytime. Cancel from your account settings and you won't be charged again. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
Is Stripe payment secure? ↓
Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe — we never store your card details. Stripe is used by millions of businesses worldwide and is PCI-DSS compliant.
